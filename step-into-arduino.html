Why Arduino?

So, you know that Arduino is easy to use. But why should you spend time learning how to use this microcontroller board? Well, it's incredibly flexible. With an Arduino, you can do everything from control a robot to manage a home automation project---and plenty besides.

About Phoenix Robotics Club's Step into Arduino

Phoenix Robotics Club took an initiative to conduct the workshop “step into Arduino” on Arduino in a practical-oriented way of learning. Regardless of what we are learning, we must learn it from the basics. So, the workshop “Step into Arduino” focuses on laying the solid foundations in Arduino with embedded C-Programming. According to say “Nail the basics first, Detail the details later,” we feel learning from the basics will help us to solve the most complex problems.
