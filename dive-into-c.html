Why to Learn C-Programming?

Programming plays a key role in all engineering fields. To get to know about programming it’s better to choose the C language as your first programming language as ”C” is the base of any programming applications. C language plays a vital role in Robotics and acts as the base for many programming languages.

About Phoenix Robotics Club's Dive into C

Phoenix Robotics Club took an initiative to conduct the workshop “Dive into C” on C-language in a practical-oriented way of learning. Regardless of what we are learning, we must learn it from the basics. So, the workshop “Dive into C” focuses on laying the solid foundations in C-Programming. According to say “Nail the basics first, Detail the details later,” we feel learning from the basics will help us to solve the most complex problems.
